FAQ

Good questions, honest answers

I can't swim. Can I still join?
Yes! Snorkeling and trial diving don't require swimming ability. You'll wear a wetsuit (which floats) and your private guide supports you the entire time — at your pace, because there's no one else's pace to follow.
Do you really speak English?
We keep communication simple and clear in English over LINE and Instagram, and our guides use easy English plus hand signals in the water — the universal language of diving. Hundreds of international guests have had a great time with us.
What should I bring?
Just swimwear (worn under your clothes), a towel, and sandals. Wetsuits, masks, fins, and all diving equipment are included. Drinks are on us.
What if the weather is bad?
Safety first, always. If sea conditions aren't right, we'll propose an alternative spot, reschedule, or cancel with no fee. Because we're private, we can often pivot to a sheltered location when big-group tours have to cancel.
Can we combine activities in one day?
Absolutely — that's our specialty. Morning snorkel + afternoon SUP, or trial dive + sunset surf. Tell us your wishlist and we'll build the day around it.
How far in advance should we book?
Since every slot is a private charter, dates fill up fast — especially April to October. Message us as soon as you know your travel dates; same-week requests are sometimes possible, so it never hurts to ask.
